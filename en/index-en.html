Project Devolution

Stylized Dinosaur Survival Simulator

Project Devolution is a dinosaur survival game in a stylized blocky style currently in active development on Roblox. Enter a dangerous prehistoric world where every dinosaur fights for survival.

About The Game

Project Devolution is a multiplayer survival simulator where players can experience the life of a dinosaur in a large open world.

Already Available In The Game:

  • dinosaur growth system
  • survival with hunger and thirst
  • player battles
  • 4 playable dinosaurs

The game continues to actively evolve and receive new content.

Development

Active Development

Project Devolution is constantly being updated. New dinosaurs, mechanics, animations and locations are regularly added and improved.

The goal of the game is to create an atmospheric and high-quality dinosaur survival simulator with its own unique style.

The game is currently in active development. Some elements may change during production.
